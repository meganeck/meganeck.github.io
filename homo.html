Homo Monstrosus

Type of Project: BFA Final Project
When: Winter 2017
Student Project

OVER TIME, CLASSIFICATIONS BASED ON RACE HAVE SHAPED SOCIETY’S CURRENT CULTURAL PERCEPTIONS. TODAY BIRACIAL INDIVIDUALS STRUGGLE NAVIGATING THROUGH STRICT DEFINITIONS OF RACE. AS THE WORLD MOVES TOWARDS GLOBALIZATION, QUANTIFYING RACE WILL BECOME OBSOLETE. CURRENT RACIAL CLASSIFICATIONS FAIL TO BE INCLUSIVE OF THE EVER CHANGING FACE OF RACE. BY THROWING AWAY OUR STRICT PERCEPTIONS OF RACE, WE CAN EMBRACE DIVERSITY AND BECOME A MORE INCLUSIVE SOCIETY.
