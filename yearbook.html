BFA Yearbook '17

Type of Project: Layout Design
When: Winter 2017
Collaborators: Haley Stoneking & Jasmine Roth
Side Project

In my design program it is a tradition for the graduating class to create and design their own graduation ceremonies. We developed a brand language that covered invites, programs, and motion graphics. We decided as a class that we wanted to create a yearbook to commemorate our "BFAmily." Myself and two other art directors took on the challenge of creating this yearbook. We gathered illustrations by our classmates, and even orchastrated a photoshoot to take portriats and a "family" photo.
